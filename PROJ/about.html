Behind The Craft

Our Story

Yogu Milktea was founded with a passion for creating high-quality milk tea that delivers both exceptional taste and customer satisfaction. We are dedicated to using only premium ingredients to ensure every drink is consistent, refreshing, and enjoyable.

Driven by the love and the overwhelming support of our early local customers, they proudly made the leap from their home kitchen to opening our very first official physical storefront in April 2023.
